FAQs

Мы составили несколько часто задаваемых вопросов, чтобы помочь вам. Если вашего вопроса нет среди них, не стесняйтесь обращаться к нам.

Какие спецификации программного обеспечения необходимы для запуска Smart TIO?
Smart TIO работает без проблем в Windows 10 и более поздних версиях. Мы не можем гарантировать, что Smart TIO будет работать в более ранних версиях Windows. Свяжитесь с нами если возникнут вопросы после загрузки нашей демоверсии here. и мы постараемся решить. Mac-версия будет выпущена очень скоро.
Доступен ли Smart TIO для моей страны?
We have created a list of countries for which Smart TIO is currently available which you can find here as well as with details about the data we are using for each module.
If you don't find your country, contact us and we will check data availability for your country. After that, we will inform you about the potential required adjustments and the price of building SMART-TIO for your country. We continue working to expand and include more countries every day.
How long can I use Smart TIO once I buy it?
Currently, we have licenses for up to two years but you can upgrade your license or request a quote if you have would like a permanent Smart-TIO license. contact with sales
Once I pay, what happen?
Our team will send you an activation code as well as a link to download the installation package. With this code you will be able to use Smart TIO for all users who you have purchased the application for.
I want to have more users, what should I do?
Sales team will be happy to propose you an offer adjusted as much as possible to your needs. contact our sales team
What if I download Smart TIO and it doesn't work on my device?
First of all try to download our demo here in order to not have future problems. If you already have paid please contact our sales team
Do I need internet to work with Smart TIO?
Although you do not need internet to work with Smart TIO, you do need internet to download and install Smart TIO.
Can AYeconomics access to my Smart-TIO simulation data?
AYeconmics cannot access your data, neither in the demo version nor once you have purchased the app.
How often is the application updated?
We update Smart Tio is any bug having detected or if data have been updated. We will send you the information of how update your version and the information about the changes.
How often is the application upgraded?
We will try to upgrade SMART-TIO yearly including new functionalities, calculations and improvements. We will send you the information explain the advantages of the new Smart-TIO and the price plan for upgrades.
