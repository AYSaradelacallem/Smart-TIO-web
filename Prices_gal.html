FAQs

Hemos creado un apartado de preguntas frecuentes que quizás te ayuden. Si tu duda no está contemplada en este apartado no dudes en

¿Qué software se necesita para usar Smart TIO?

Smart TIO funciona sin problemas en Windows 10 y superior. No podemos garantizar que Smart TIO funcione en versiones inferiores de Windows. Si tienes alguna duda tras , descargar nuestra demo siempre puedes con nuestro equipo.

Mac version will be coming out very soon.

¿Está disponible Smart TIO en mi país?

We have created a list of countries for which Smart TIO is currently available which you can find here as well as with details about the data we are using for each module.
If you do not find your country, contact us and we will check data availability for your country. After that, we will inform you about the potential required adjustments and the price of building Smart TIO for your country. We continue working to expand and include more countries every day.

¿Durante cuánto tiempo puedo usar Smart TIO una vez lo compre?

Currently, we have licenses for up to two years but you can upgrade your license or request a quote if you would like to have a permanent Smart TIO license. contact with sales

Una vez realice el pago ¿Qué debo hacer?

Our team will send you an activation code as well as a link to download the installation package. With this code you will be able to use Smart TIO for all users who you have purchased the application for.

Me gustaría poder disponer de más usuarios ¿Qué debo hacer?

Sales team will be happy to propose you an offer adjusted as much as possible to your needs. contact our sales team

¿Qué pasa si descargo Smart TIO pero no funciona en mi dispositivo?

First of all try to download our demo here in order not to have issues in the future. If you have already paid, please contact our sales team

¿Necesito internet para poder trabajar con Smart TIO?

Although you do not need internet to work with Smart TIO, you do need internet to download and install Smart TIO.

¿Puede AYeconomics tener acceso a los datos de mi simulación?

AYeconomics cannot access your data, neither in the demo version nor once you have purchased the app.

How often is the application updated?

We update Smart TIO if any bug is detected or data have been updated. We will send you the information on how to update your version and the information about the changes.

How often is the application upgraded?

We will try to upgrade Smart TIO yearly including new functionalities, calculations and improvements. We will send you the information explaining the advantages of the new Smart TIO and the price plan for upgrades.
