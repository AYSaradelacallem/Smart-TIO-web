FAQs

We have made some frequently asked questions to help you. If your question is not among them do not hesitate to contact us.

How do I know if my computers are Smart TIO supported?
Easy! Download our demo here. This way you will be able to see if Smart TIO will work on your device and you can also see Smart TIO in action.
Is Smart TIO available in my country?
We have created a list of countries for which Smart TIO is available which you can find here.
If you don't find your country in the list we suggest you to contact us, maybe we can find a way to work together. We continue working to expand and include more countries every day.
How long can I use Smart TIO once I buy it?
Currently we have licenses for up to two years but you can always upgrade your license! In any case, if you have any special request you can always contact with sales
Once I pay, what happen?
Our team will send you a link to download the instalation package. You'll recibe an activation code too. With this code you will be able to use Smart TIO for all users for whom you have purchased the application.
I need to have more users, what should I do?
At Smart TIO we have a sales team that will be happy to answer any questions you may have and to adjust as much as possible to your needs. contact our sales team
What if I download Smart TIO and then it doesn't work on my device?
First of all try to download our demo here in order to not have future problems. If you already have paid please contact our sales team
Do I need internet to work with Smart TIO?
Although you do not need internet to work with Smart TIO, you do need internet to download and install Smart TIO.
Can AYeconomics access to my demo data?
AYeconmics cannot access your demo data, nor your Smart TIO calculation data once you purchase the app.
How often is the application data updated?
Approximately every year, coinciding with the data update, we launch an update of the application with new functionalities and improvements. A link will be sent to you so that you can download the new version.
How is the sector data updated?
Depending on the statistical agencies from which the data that nourish the application are extracted
