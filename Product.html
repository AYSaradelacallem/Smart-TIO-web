Smart TIO

Perform calculations in an easy way

Smart TIO is a software created to facilitate economic decisions by helping to perform calculations in a simple, fast and, ultimately, efficient way. Smart Tio saves the team time and helps to make economic decisions and achieve objectives in an optimal way thanks to the use of mathematical calculations, such as the hypothetical extraction of sectors or dddd, adapted for easy and simple understanding. Thanks to the data export facility you will be able to work by uploading information from your own files and, if you consider it appropriate, you will be able to enter or modify the data manually. Of course you can export the data. Thanks to Smart Tio you can generate reports that will save you time as well as export graphs to support your own reports or presentations.

Smart Tio

Eco

More green, more eco

Add the eco module to Smart TIO and get the possibility to work with values and sectors that are important for the economy and the ecological process. No doubt a great tool that will greatly support you in the monitoring of sectors so important for the economy and general welfare.

From here you can manage data such as water, gas emissions and specific energy sectors.
