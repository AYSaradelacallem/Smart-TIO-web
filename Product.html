Smart TIO

Perform calculations in an easy way

Smart TIO is a software created to facilitate economic decisions by helping to perform impact calculations in a simple, fast, precise and, ultimately, efficient way. Smart TIO saves time by calculating simulations based on input-ouput models adjusted to your own economy. Smart-tio allow to simulate the introduction of a policy, or the impact of global trends (covid-19, war etc.) You can type data easily to do the simulations or use your on csv or xlxs file by using the import feature. It includes results on economic, labour and environmental variables ensuring more sustainable decisions. Results are shown using figures to visualice the results easily and fast. You can download the figures as well as a pdf report to have a better understanding of the main results.

Smart Tio

Eco

More green, more eco

Add the eco module to Smart-TIO and get environmental results in addition to the economic. The eco-model includes carbon emissions by sector as well as energy use for different types distinguishing renewables and non-renewables such a coal, petroleum product among others. This is a great tool to support economic decisions with the minor damage to the environment increasing the general welfare.

Take into account the environment in your decisions and improve population and planet health increasing the long-run welfare.

Smart Tio

Social

More social, more inclusive

Add the social module to Smart TIO to take in account the labour effects of your simulations. Decisions to a more sustainable economy should take into considerations not only from economic and environmental results but also social effects. Smart-tio social module simulates direct and indirect results on labour-market. Take into account in which sector jobs are created or destroyed which changes in your economy to keep a healthy labour-market and the welfare of your population.

Take into account the labour-market in your decisions and improve the live of the population.

Smart Tio

Regional

More local, more nearby

Consider the regional smart-tio to be able to do the demand simulations for your country and each of the regions. Regions are specialised in different products and sectors and, as a result, the demand simulations would have different effects depending on the region.

Take into account the regional aspects and reduce the spacial inequalities.
