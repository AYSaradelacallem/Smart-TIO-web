Tutoriales

Primera vez con Smart TIO

Paso a paso Smart TIO nos guía. El primer paso es seleccionar entre realizar un analísis a nivel o regional. Tan pronto como selecciones el tipo de análisis que deseas realizar serás dirigido a la pantalla de cálculo.

Cálculo de demanda de impacto

The impact demand analysis provides estimations on direct economics effects from changes in final demand and indirect effects caused by ... the changes in the production of the suppliers of interrelated sectors.At the calculation screen, you can type value manually or import values and change the demand component that you are interested in,such as households or government consumption, gross capital formation, exports and etc.

Cálculos de extracción hipotética

Los análisis de extracción hipotética estiman la importancia de un sector en particular de la economía. quantifies how much the total output, taxes or employment would change (decrease), if this sector would disappear in the economy. In menu bar you can select a sector for estimation and results will be shown automatically.

Exportar resutados

Los resultados de los cálculos pueden ser visualizados y descargados. Los resultados se pueden visualizar por totales o por sectores. También es posible generar un PDF descargable con la descripción de las estimaciones y las interpretaciones de tus resultados.

Impact demand calculations

The impact demand analysis provides estimations on direct economics effects from changes in final demand and indirect effects caused by the changes in the production of the suppliers of interrelated sectors. Hypothetical extraction calculations

Hypothetical extraction analysis estimates the importance of a particular sector in the economy and quantifies how much the total output, taxes or employment would change (decrease), if this sector would disappear in the economy. In menu bar you can select a sector for estimation and results will be shown automatically.
