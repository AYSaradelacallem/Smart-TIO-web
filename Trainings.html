Tutorials

First time opening Smart TIO

Go step by step throughout smart-tio guide process and choose to run national or regional analysis. As long as you choose the type of analysis you want to perform you will enter in the calculations display.

Impact demand calculations

The impact demand calculates the indirect effects corresponding to the production of the suppliers required to produce in addition to the direct impact resulting from changes in final demand components. Enter in the calculation screen, type data or import values and change the demand component that your are interested such households, tourism or government consumption as well as investments or exports.

Hypothetical extraction calculations

With hypothetical extraction approach you can estimate the importance of a particular sector of the economy and quantify how much the total output, taxes or labour would change (decrease) if this sector would disappear from the economy. Enter in the hypothetical extraction calculations screen and select a sector. Results will be shown automatically.

Export results

Visualise and download your total results in figures and tables as well as by sectors. Press the save icon to download and save the figure in your computer. You can also download and save an automatic PDF report in your computer to have an explanation of your main results.
